The HHS Music Parents Organization

The Hamden High School Music Parents Organization is a non-profit, parent-led organization that supports the Hamden High Band, Orchestra, and Choral Music programs. The mission of the HHSMPO is to foster a wider interest in music study and Music Department activities by promoting and supporting the Music Department through fundraising and material support.

Throughout the year we support our students and their teachers by fundraising money for programs and to help students pay for apparel, trips, and equipment, as well as volunteering as pit crew for the Marching Band and chaperones on trips.

If you are interested in serving on the Board of Directors this year (we need a choir parent!) please contact Beth Young at eyoung@hamden.org

*We will need parents from choir, band, AND orchestra to volunteer for the Board next year!*

If you are interested in volunteering for the spring concerts or fundraisers please complete this form.

Even if you don't have the time to join, we are here to represent YOU and would love to hear your suggestions and thoughts for supporting Hamden music.
