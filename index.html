The HHS Music Parents Organization

The Hamden High School Music Parents Organization is a non-profit, parent-led organization that supports the Hamden High Band, Orchestra, and Choral Music programs.

Throughout the year we raise funds for programs, apparel, trips, and equipment, as well as volunteering as pit crew for the Marching Band and chaperones on trips.

If you are interested in serving on the Board of Directors this year please contact Bob Gribbon at bobgribbon61@gmail.com

*We will need parents from choir, band, AND orchestra to volunteer for the Board next year!*

Even if you don't have the time to join, we are here to represent YOU and would love to hear your suggestions and thoughts for supporting Hamden music.
